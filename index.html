Business Analysis

This time i was playing a crucial role between the different stakeholders in software develepment, e.g. business decision makers, testing, development and devops teams. It was to take the coordination between all the stakegholders at next level, as it was relatively less of transferring of information between all of them, which unltimately bringing the entire project objectives down.

I implemented several techniques to bring the harmony between the teams. Started with brainstrorming with many crucial stakeholders and getting to know about them and their perscpective about the project. Circulated surveys and conducted interviews to grasp more about business objctive from everyones perspective. Then later, I sat down and executed a GAP analysis at my level and later continuous presentations and colloborations created a trust level among teams. We were able to make the impact and brought more harmony in team.
